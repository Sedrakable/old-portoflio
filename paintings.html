Painting Projects

Painting is something I did when I was little, I even took weekly classes, but in never really followed me. The skill was mostly acquired through my college journey. Similar to drawing, I learned about visualization, sizing, lighting, shadows, types of strokes, color pallets and more. At the end of it all, I understood that the final product is less about detail and more about getting the feel of the scene. There definitely is more to come!
