Origami Projects

Origami was my first form of art. Like most kids, I once folded a paper airplane, but it did not stop there. I've spent most of my free time between the ages of 10 and 13 folding origami. The Origami styles included Modular, Animals, Tessellation, etc... This form of art taught me about precision and patience since these are key for any great final product.
