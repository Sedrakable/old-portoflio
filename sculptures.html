Scupting Projects

Sculpting is more or less a new art form for me. There is for sure more to come in this section.

Red Hood Helmet

This is a recreation of a helmet of a fictional character called the Red Hood, for Holloween. Using kids foam puzzles and a glue-gun, I cut-out individual pieces and put all together to create the shape. Then I cleaned up the edges and spray painted the whole mask red. I had to recreate the mask 3 times because the first time the mask came out too small for my head, the second time it came out too big.
Size: Normal head-shape
Date: 2015

Tower Bridge base

This is a popsicle stick recreation of the London Tower Bridge base with a moving deck. This was made as a school project, but I intend to recreate or to finish the whole bridge.
Size: 18" x 24"
Date: 2017

Wide Ice Bear

This is my first sculpture in 10 year. Its a sculpture of a Polar Bear for the cartoon "We Bare Bears". Using sculpey clay I molded and baked the model, then, using acrilic paint, I painted the whole piece. I wanted to sculpt something easy as a starter. Turned out pretty clean.
Size: 10cm x 7cm x 4.2cm
Date: 2019

Christmas Zac
